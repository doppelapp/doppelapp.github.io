What is Doppel?

They say that there are between 4 and 7 people in the world who look exactly like you.

Doppel is an app that helps you find these people -- your "doppelgangers" -- in the easiest way possible.

Plus, we'll notify you if and when someone matches with you, so even if you don't find your doppelganger the first time, you can wait for them to find you!

Submit your photos, create an account, and view your doppelgangers. It takes less than a minute.

How Doppel Works

Doppel uses a custom Convulutional Neural Network (CNN) to generate image embeddings from the photos you send, and then compares your image embeddings to those of other users, actors, presidents, etc. Image embeddings with high similarities to each other indicate look-a-likes.

To speed up this process, Doppel also uses a Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM) to classify users into "sets" based on the images you set. This way, the algorithm only needs to compare your images to those who belong to your set, rather than all the users, presidents, etc.
